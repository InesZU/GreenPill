About Our App

Welcome to GreenPill, your personal assistant Sina, designed to guide you in exploring natural remedies customized to your age, gender, medical conditions, and allergies. Our app provides a gateway to discovering herbal treatments for common health concerns, drawing from the rich wisdom of Chinese, Ayurvedic, and Unani medicine traditions. Whether you’re seeking relief from headaches, digestive issues, or sleep disturbances, GreenPill offers personalized suggestions that cater specifically to your unique needs.

Powered by cutting-edge AI technology, GreenPill uses OpenAI’s advanced language model to deliver comprehensive insights and recommendations on natural remedies. This integration allows us to tap into a vast repository of global medicinal knowledge, combining traditional practices with modern understanding for well-rounded and informed suggestions.

Always consult your healthcare provider before trying any new remedy, especially if you have existing health conditions or are on medication.

GreenPill is not responsible for any risks or adverse effects associated with the use of remedies suggested by the app. Please ensure you input your allergies and medical conditions upon registration to receive the most accurate suggestions.

Keep in mind that AI recommendations can sometimes be inaccurate, so always double-check the information provided and do your research before taking action.

Allergies

Why they matter

Allergies play a crucial role in determining the remedies suggested by this app. Some herbs may cause allergic reactions, so it’s important to list any known allergies when using the app. Doing so ensures that the remedies recommended are safe for you to try.

Health Conditions

Impact on remedy suggestions

Certain medical conditions or medications can affect the suitability of natural remedies. It’s important to provide details of your health conditions when using the app, so we can tailor suggestions accordingly. Always check with your healthcare provider if you’re unsure.
